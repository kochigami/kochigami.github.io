Research

About my research
I am interested in an interactive robot which can interact with people.
Such kind of a robot is expected to be the existence of someone who has their own personality, rather than just a machine.
Especially, I focus on children and have been considering two questions below:
- What is important for a robot in order to become “someone” for children?
- How do children treat a robot?
I have been searching for these questions by designing software and hardware for a robot and evaluating them in field trials.
My research goal is creating a robot which meditates interaction between children and other people by enriching interaction between children and a robot.
[Research Areas]
human-robot interaction (HRI), child-robot interaction (CRI), social robotics
[Research Interests]
behavior design, group interaction, field trial, science communication
